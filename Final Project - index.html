Famous Chips Described in Haiku

Pringles

Man with a moustache
Paraboloids piled up
Single? Eat Pringles

Doritos

Triangle shaped
Finger-lickin’ good (I guess?)
It’s a gamer’s chip!

Lays

The salty classic
The flavour competition
The forever pick

Ms. Vickies

The kettle cooked,
colour coordinated,
loved recipe!

Cheetos

Crunchy or puffy,
cheesy or spicy goodness,
they go CRUNCH, CRUNCH, CRUNCH!

Ruffles

The forgettable
NBA’s official chip.
“Lays wannabe?” …“Yeah.”

Takis

Famously spicy.
Highschoolers’ beloved.
Canada’s baby.

Tostitos

“Where’s the dip, bro?”
“In the fridge, go get it now!”
Tostitos for life!
